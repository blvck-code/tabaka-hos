Maternity

The NHWC is located within the confines of the Nairobi Hospital complex. It houses several clinics which include:

  • Antenatal and maternity services with feto -maternal medicine specialization
  • General gynecology services
  • Menopausal medicine
  • Minimally invasive and gynaecological surgery services
ANTENATAL AND MATERNITY SERVICES

The Tabaka Mission hospital provides top of the range antenatal, labour and maternity services under one roof. We provide antenatal services and packages suited to ones needs. The Anderson maternity package is run in the Anderson hospital centre located adjacent to the doctor’s plaza. It is open to all and is run by qualified obstetrician gynaecologist supported by a team of midwives and nurses who will support your medical and social needs from before conception to delivery and thereafter in care of the baby.

Antenatal & Maternity Packages FAQ’S

  1. What does the Antenatal Package cover?
    • 6 Antenatal visits (any extra visits out of the schedule will be charges separately)
    • 2 Postnatal visit by the doctor after CS at 2 weeks & 6 weeks
    • 1 Neo-natal baby visit (at 2 weeks old)
    • Pre-natal investigations i.e. Laboratory tests & 2 ultrasound scans
    • 1 postnatal visit by the doctor (after Normal delivery at 6 weeks)
    • 1 physiotherapy session
  2. What is the price of the Antenatal Package?
    • Ksh. 60,000
  3. What does the Normal Delivery Package cover?
    • Obstetricians, Paediatricians & Resident Registrar fee
